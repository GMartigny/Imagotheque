What am I looking at ?

Imagine an infinite museum. In it, each painting is 500 pixels wide, 300 pixels tall and completely unique.

Each pixel can take any "web" color. A "web" color is defined by 3 component (red, green and blue) that each have 256 possible value. Which means that 3256 equals about 16 Millions possible colors. With 150 000 pixels per painting, the total number of possibilities is about 9x10270926.

In the end, our museum is huge, but finite. We can scour it for years and never see the end, but every 500 by 300 images are in here. There's a picture of you somewhere, an all green canvas, a formula for nuclear fusion's reactors ...
